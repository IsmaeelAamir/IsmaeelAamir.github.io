Bootcamp 2020: Project

My project is a fun little game where you use a small blue ball to pop the green balls, whilst avoiding the red balls. Once all of the green balls have been popped, you win and can play another round. The size, position and ratio of red to green balls is random. This project makes particular use of arrays, loops and randomness. An array is used to store data about each ball, and a loop is used to populate the array, with random values for the size, positon and colour. The ball bounces off of the walls, and also bounces off the red balls. But if you hit a red ball, you lose a life, out of five lives. When the ball bounces off these objects, or pops a green ball, a noise is played.
